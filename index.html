About Topview

History & Mission

Founded in 2016, Topview Private School started with 15 students and a vision to create a centre of excellence.

Mission: To be one of the global institutions that produce world-class quality education with technology, innovative ideals, and skills development.

Vision: To be the leading light in holistic education, raising generations that transform society.
